My audience is neovim users who like customizing their config.

About

Hey there! This is a website to help people find new and exciting neovim colorschemes! ON you homepage, you can scroll through our list of the most popular neovim colorschemes. You are welcome to use the searchbar to find a colorscheme that you are looking for though.

Hello from the creator! My name is Troy. I am a software engineer who has experience with HTML, CSS, TS, Python, and C#. I love learning about new developer tools, and I love open source projects. To help share my passion for software projects like this, I decided to make this site of different Neovim colorschemes. Here you can find a new colorscheme that fits your Neovim setup.

Wireframes

Home

Each colorscheme will have the name of the colorscheme, the url for the colorscheme, description, tags, and compatibility listed beside the image of the colorscheme. Also, the first time the user visits the page, they will see all of the colorschemes instead of just one.

About Page

At the bottom of the page, there will be a link to the neovim website, and there will be a link to my github.
